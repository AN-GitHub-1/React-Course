199. What's So Complex About Forms?

1. Forms and inputs can assume different states.
    - One or more inputs are invalid
        - Output input specific error messages
        - Ensure form can't be submitted
    - All inputs are valid 

2. When to validate?
    - When form is submitted
    - When a input is losing focus
    - On every keystroke
